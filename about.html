About Bar 10

Downtown Riverton's elevated night-out spot — serious food, serious drinks, and a room that feels like it belongs right here in County Ten.

Who We Are

Built for Riverton.

Bar 10 sits on Broadway in the heart of downtown Riverton — a bar and restaurant that takes food and drinks seriously without taking itself too seriously.

The food is built around specialty artisan pizzas with names that mean something to anyone from Fremont County — the Popo Agie, the Wiggins Fork, the Big Horn BBQ. The appetizer spread is deep enough to make it hard to order just one thing. Salads that hold their own. And the bar goes further than you'd expect: craft cocktails, a solid margarita lineup, dessert martinis, and Wyoming beer on tap that rotates.

This isn't a chain. It doesn't try to be anything it isn't. It's a Riverton spot — built for the people here, and for anyone passing through who wants a better night than they planned on.

Trivia every Thursday. Live music on weekends. The kind of atmosphere where you end up staying longer than you expected.

County Ten

County Ten is how people here refer to Fremont County, Wyoming — big sky, river bottom, the kind of community that sticks. Bar 10 is proud to be part of that. We think you can get a serious pizza and a craft cocktail out here, and we've built this place to prove it.
