About Bar 10

A downtown Riverton original. Good food, real drinks, and a room that feels like it belongs right here in County Ten.

Who We Are

Built for Riverton

Bar 10 sits right on Broadway in downtown Riverton — a bar and restaurant that takes food and drinks seriously without taking itself too seriously.

The food is built around specialty artisan pizzas with names that mean something to anyone who knows Fremont County — the Popo Agie, the Wiggins Fork, the Big Horn BBQ. The appetizer menu is broad enough to make it hard to order just one thing. The salads hold their own. And the bar program runs deep: craft cocktails, a solid margarita lineup, dessert martinis, and Wyoming beer on tap that rotates to stay fresh.

This isn't a chain, and it doesn't try to be. It's a Riverton spot for Riverton people — and for anyone passing through who wants a good meal and a better drink than they expected to find.

Thursday trivia nights draw regulars and newcomers alike. Weekend live music brings the energy up. And the atmosphere throughout the week is the kind of place where you end up staying longer than you planned.

County Ten

"Raising the Bar in County Ten"

County Ten is more than a slogan. It's how people here refer to Fremont County, Wyoming — the place with the big sky, the river bottom, and the kind of community that sticks together. Bar 10 is proud to be part of that. We think you can get a serious pizza and a craft cocktail out here, and we've built this place to prove it.
